    • Other Services

    Rosario Bridal Boutique ensures that your experience is the best it can be when choosing your wedding dress, so instead of most 1 hour dress appointments we like to give you that extra time with each appointment being 1 hour and 30 minutes. We understand that choosing a dress can be an overwhelming experience, so you are more than welcome to bring a maximum of two people with you to your appointment.

    Shoes & Accessories

    At Rosario Bridal Boutique we can provide shoes for you to use when trying on your selection of dresses during your appointment. However, you are more than welcome to bring your own shoes if you have already picked them out or have another similar pair at home. Top tip: we do suggest a similar height to the ones you intend to wear on the day.

    VIP Appointment

    We do offer VIP appointments which includes:

    • A bottle of bubbly (which you can either drink or take with you!)
    • Luxury canapes - vegetarian/vegan options available
    • A longer 2 hour appointment to try on more dresses
    • Photo props for extra laughs
    • £50 voucher towards your dress
    • Free dress storage
    • 2 extra guests at your appointment (1 bride & 4 guests)

    Seamstress Services

    Need some alterations to your dress? No problem, we work closely with a team of local seamstresses who can do home visits or will be more than happy for you to book an alteration appointment at their studio. You will be given all this information in your aftercare package with your dress or you can ask for it instore.

    In addition to your wedding dress, if any of your dresses or groomsman outfits need any alterations they are happy to accommodate at the following fees:

    • Bridesmaids dress: £25 each
    • Flower girl dress: £10 each
    • Page boy outfit: £10 each
    • Groomsman outfit re-hemming: £5 per cuff
    • Groomsman outfit larger alterations: TBD
    • Groom alterations: TBD (appointment only)
