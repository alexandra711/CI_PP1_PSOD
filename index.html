About Us

What's our history?

Started in 1970 in Eastbourne, Park School of Dancing was opened by former professional dancer Sue Wood with the hopes of sharing her love of dance with the local community.

Who can take class?

Anyone can take class at Park School of Dancing, we have a range of different classes to suit everyone from toddlers to adults.

Do you perform?

Yes we do! Children and adults have the opportunity to compete in dance competitions in solos, duets, trios and groups in a variety of different styles. We also do shows to showcase the School's talent, this includes selected dancers being included in local Pantomime and performances.
